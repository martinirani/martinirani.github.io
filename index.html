About me

Hello there! I'm a Neuroscience graduate student working under the supervision of Dr. Sepideh Sadaghiani at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

I'm interested in investigating the neural network dynamics that underlie goal-directed behavior and cognitive control. I also have a longtime interest in causal reasoning and the evolution of cognition.

Before Illinois, I received my BSc. in Biochemistry from Universidad de Chile and worked as a technical associate at the Interdisciplinary Center for Neuroscience at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile.
